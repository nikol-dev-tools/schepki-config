Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all browser extensions developed by Schepki Dev (the "Extensions"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Our Extensions collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to help us improve the product. This includes:

  • Extension open/close events (how often the extension is used).
  • Button click events (e.g., which features are most popular).
  • General geographic region (country level only, derived from browser language settings).

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) via the Measurement Protocol to collect this data. All data is anonymous — we do not collect IP addresses, names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history.
  • We do not collect passwords or login credentials.
  • We do not collect any content from web pages you visit.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Remote Configuration

Our Extensions fetch a remote configuration file (JSON) from GitHub to dynamically update donation links, promotional banners, and affiliate links. This file contains no personal data and is publicly accessible. The Extension only reads this configuration — it does not send any personal data to GitHub.

4. Affiliate Links

Some Extensions may display affiliate links (e.g., to Amazon). If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We clearly label all affiliate links.

5. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained by Google Analytics for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can disable analytics collection by using browser extensions that block Google Analytics.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
